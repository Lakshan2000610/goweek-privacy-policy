Privacy Policy for GoWeek

Effective Date: 2026-01-21

GoWeek (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the GoWeek mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

When you sign in using Google, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (if available)

This information is used only to identify you and personalize your experience.

b. App Usage Data

We collect data related to app usage, such as:

  • Tasks you create
  • Task completion and missed status
  • Time spent on tasks
  • Task categories

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate users using Google Sign-In
  • Sync tasks across devices
  • Show weekly, monthly, and yearly summaries
  • Improve app performance and user experience
We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google Firebase. We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

4. Notifications and Reminders

GoWeek uses notifications to remind you of scheduled tasks and missed tasks. You can disable notifications at any time from your device or app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

GoWeek uses third-party services such as:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Database, Analytics)
  • Google Sign-In

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

6. Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data by removing your account from the app or by contacting us using the email address below. All related data will be permanently deleted.

7. Children’s Privacy

GoWeek is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
